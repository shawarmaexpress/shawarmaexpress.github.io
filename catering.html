Catering & Group Deals

Our catering options are two: Trays or Sandwich Platters.

Sandwich platters are a mixed assortment of our sandwiches dressed with the traditional toppings and cut in half (optional). Garlic sauce can be replaced with hummus in the sandwiches or served separately.

Trays are deep containers filled with garlic potatoes, steamed rice, beef and chicken shawarma meats (or falafel in the vegan option). They come with pita bread and sides of: fattoush salad + hummus + tabbouli + pickles & turnips + garlic sauce.

Kindly allow a 2 hour notice for party platters and party trays.
